Privacy Policy

KEXP Player (Unofficial) — the website at davidpuerto.com/kexp and its browser extension. Last updated June 7, 2026.

The short version

There are no accounts, no names, no email addresses, no analytics, no ads, and no tracking. Your playlist is keyed to a random ID that says nothing about who you are. That's the whole model.

What is stored

  • A device ID. On first use, the player generates a random identifier (a UUID) and keeps it in your browser's local storage. It is not derived from anything about you or your device — it's a coin flip with 122 heads.
  • Your playlist. Songs you heart — artist, title, album, airdate, artwork URL — plus any personal notes and the order you arrange them in, stored against that random ID in a database hosted by Supabase (in the United States).
  • Aggregate counts. A heart also bumps that song's anonymous global like count, which everyone sees.

What is never collected

No name, email, IP-address log, location, browsing history, fingerprint, cookie-based profile, or anything else. The player has no idea who you are, and neither do I.

Where the player talks to

  • api.kexp.org — what's playing right now (KEXP's public API, used with KEXP's permission)
  • kexp.streamguys1.com — the live audio stream
  • jodgbwwnbrotuceanghk.supabase.co — your playlist, keyed by your random device ID
  • en.wikipedia.org — short artist summaries for the song you're looking at

The browser extension

The extension talks to the same four services and stores the same playlist data. It runs a small script on exactly one page — davidpuerto.com/kexp — whose only job is to share the site's device ID with the extension, so hearts from your toolbar and hearts from the site land in the same playlist. It does not read, modify, or observe any other website.

Your data, your call

  • Clearing your browser's site data forgets the device ID — the player starts fresh, and the old playlist is no longer reachable from your browser.
  • Want the stored rows actually deleted? Email me@davidpuerto.com and I'll remove them.

Not affiliated with KEXP

This is an independent fan project. KEXP kindly allows use of their public API, but they don't operate this player and aren't responsible for it. If you love what they do, donate to KEXP.
